# unrot Brand Kit

## Brand Essence
- **Name:** unrot
- **Tagline:** Escape the Scroll, Reclaim Your Focus
- **Voice:** sharp, playful, anti-doomscroll, intelligent, minimal fluff
- **Personality:** neo-brutalist, internet-native, slightly rebellious, depth-first

## Color Palette
Derived from the current UI:
- **Background:** #FFF9F0
- **Ink:** #1A1A1A
- **Primary:** #FFD600
- **Secondary:** #00E0FF
- **Accent:** #FF4D00
- **Muted:** #8A8A8A

## Typography
- **Display:** Space Grotesk
- **Body:** Outfit
- **Mono:** JetBrains Mono

## Visual Rules
- Use uppercase for headlines and CTA labels
- Prefer thick borders, hard shadows, and flat color blocks
- Keep layouts bold and mobile-first
- Use large type and short copy
- Let one message dominate each asset

## Copy Tone Examples
- Good: "UNROT YOUR BRAIN."
- Good: "DEPTH OVER DOOMSCROLLING."
- Good: "YOUR NEXT RABBIT HOLE AWAITS."
- Avoid: "Unlock productivity synergy"
- Avoid: "Optimize your cognitive workflow"
